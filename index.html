About Me

Hey there, I'm Sachin Pavale

Welcome to my website!

I'm a multi-disciplinary designer with a passion for crafting exceptional user experiences. I currently work as a Creative Graphic Designer in a freelance basis, and I'm actively expanding my skills in UI Design and front-end Development.

My expertise includes converting designs into responsive HTML5/CSS3, a process I fondly refer to as "PSD to HTML Development." I'm always eager to learn new skills and stay updated on the latest design trends. I frequently explore online resources and attend industry workshops and events.

I view the world through a lens of visual experiences and thrive on taking on new challenges and opportunities. If you believe I can contribute to your projects, I'd love to hear from you. My background as a graphic designer has prepared me to make a valuable impact on any new challenge.

My Skills & Experience

I have 2.5 years of experience in Graphic Design, specializing in creating a wide range of marketing materials. My work includes designing website templates, web banners, logos, social media posts, carousels, print materials such as billboards, roll-ups, flyers, posters, food menu cards, invitation cards, ID cards, business cards, labels, and stickers, product packaging etc.

I am proficient in using industry-standard design software like Adobe Creative Suite, which includes Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign, as well as CorelDRAW to bring these projects to life. This is why I try to make each project creative and submit it to the client within the deadline.
